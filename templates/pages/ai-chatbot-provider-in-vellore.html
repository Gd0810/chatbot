FAQ about AI Chatbot

How do I train the AI chatbot with my own data?

Training the AI Chatbot

  • Login to your chatbot dashboard using the company-provided credentials. Then click on AI Knowledge → Knowledge Base and choose the source type (plain text or JSON). Input your data and add it to the knowledge base.

  • Next, go to Qdrant.tech , register for an account, and create a cluster. You will receive a cluster API key and an endpoint.

  • Use these credentials in Account → Knowledge Configuration, save the settings, and that’s it—you’ve successfully created your bot’s knowledge base with your data.

    • How the AI Chatbot Website Crawler Works?

    Training the AI Chatbot

  • You simply provide your website URL to the Website Crawler.

  • The crawler automatically fetches data from your website.

  • It extracts the important content and site URLs and presents them in plain text format.

  • You can then copy and paste this data directly into your knowledge base.

  • This process reduces your workload, since you don’t have to manually create or input content yourself.

    How do I change colors, fonts, and font sizes for the AI chatbot?

    Customizing the AI Chatbot Interface

    Go to Bot Styling → Customize Settings. From there, you can select your brand colors (chatbot color and background color), choose a Google Font from the given list, and adjust the font sizes.
